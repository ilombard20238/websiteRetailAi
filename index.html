What is RetailAI?

RetailAI is a computer vision system trained using AlexNet to identify store merchandise—bags, hats, shoes, and more. When an item is missing a price tag, RetailAI instantly recognizes the product from a photo and retrieves the correct price.

How It Works

  1. Take a photo of the item.
  2. RetailAI processes the image using deep learning.
  3. The system displays the product name and price instantly.
